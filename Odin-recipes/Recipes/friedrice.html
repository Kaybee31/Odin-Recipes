Ingredients

  • 3 cups of cooked rice
  • Sesame oil
  • 1 small white chopped onion
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots thawed.
  • 2-3 Tablespoons soy sauce more or less to taste
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoon chopped green onions(optional)

Preparation

  1. Chop and prepare the ingredients
  2. Season the meat and prepare the rice
  3. Mix the ingredients
  4. Add soy sauce,oyster sauce, and salt to taste
  5. Fried rice can be served with frfied chicken,beef or sausage
