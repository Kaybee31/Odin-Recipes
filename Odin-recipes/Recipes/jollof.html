Jollof Rice

The origins of Jollof rice can be traced to the 1300s in the ancient Wolof Empire (also called the Jolof Empire), which spanned parts of today's Senegal, The Gambia and Mauritania

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Mackerel
  • Salad
  • Oil

Preparation

  1. Put a saucepan on fire and add oil
  2. Cut some onions into the hot oil and stir
  3. Add tomato paste and your grinded pepper and onion and allow it to cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add mackerel to the sauce,and pour cups of rice needed to be cooked to the sauce
  5. You can add bay leaves and other spices in this process
  6. Pour cups of water into the saucepan and allow to cook for 45 minutes
  7. The Jollof is ready and can be served with beef,meat,egg or sausage.
